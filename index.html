Education Background


Yifeng has strong academic backgroud in both Chemistry and Physics, with his GPA ranks 1st in Department of Chemical Physics (1/52), and also top in School of Chemistry and Materials Science (3/206). Because of his outstanding academic performance, he has been awarded for Guo Moruo Scholarship, the highest honor for undergraduates in USTC, and National Scholarship, the highest honor for undergraduates in PR China.

During the summer of 2015, Yifeng attened Cross-disciplinary Scholars in Science and Technology (CSST) research program held by UCLA. He was awared for Best Presentation and Outstanding Research Award (top 3 students in Chemistry Seminar). He also hosted the Cross-disciplinary Scholars (CDS) Leture by Prof. Paul S. Weiss.

Yifeng now has been admitted to MIT, Department of Chemistry, and will go there this autumn.
